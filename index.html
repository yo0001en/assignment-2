Canvas Training is not just a regular gym. It speaks of a progressive fitness journey, and how the gym charts it alongside you. Hence the name Canvas.

Class in Canvas training mix CrossFit with StrongFit (the principles of safe, proper movement.) The vibrant colours - purple, orange and pink used all around the gym represent power fruits - acai, papaya, guava - that nourish the body and mind. The spring in the logo represents more than the muscle contraction when you’re working out.

Canvas promises Top-to-toe fitness that's fun, functional and different every time you return!
